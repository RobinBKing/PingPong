Ping Pong

  • About Ping Pong
  • Created by:
  • Rob King
  • GitHub URL:
  • https://github.com/RobinBKing/PingPong.git
  • Description:
  • A study in Behavior Driven Development, (BDD). The program switches out numbers multiple by 3, 5 and 15 with the words 'Ping', 'Pong' or 'Ping Pong'
  • Technology used:

  • Application: JavaScript, jQuery, HTML5, CSS3, bootstrap

    Testing: Mocha, Chai

  • Leagal:
  • Copyright (c) 2016 Robin B. King

Want to play?

Here how it works:

  1. Numbers that can be divided by 3 get a 'Ping'
  2. Numbers that can be divided 5 get a 'Pong'
  3. Numbers that can be divided by 15 get a 'Ping Pong'
